Who We Are

The Truth Movement - Mission Statement The Truth Movement exists to show that no matter your past, your pain, or your mistakes, you can rise up, speak your truth, and change your life. We believe God is everything — in us, around us, and working through us — and with faith, hard work, and honesty, transformation is possible for anyone. We stand for second chances, for speaking life into the broken, for guiding young people away from the streets and toward their true purpose. Through education, creativity, community, and raw truth, we help people rewrite their story — because your story ain’t over, it’s just getting started. The Truth Movement is not about judgment. It's about love, respect, and real accountability. We lift each other up, we own our past, and we push each other to become something greater — for ourselves, our families, and our communities. Our truth. Our time. Our movement.

Our Programs

Redemption Academy

For people coming home from prison or caught in the street cycle — mentorship, education, job training, and spiritual growth. A real shot at something better.

Young Kings & Queens

Work with young people in the community — sports, music, writing, business skills — teaching them discipline, confidence, and how to grind with purpose.

Sober & Free Network

A real support system for sobriety.

Bars & Books

Creative programs mixing music, poetry, and education. Write your truth, rap your truth, read your truth — heal through expression.

Truth Scholarship Fund

Helping folks chase education, no matter their age.
